Delete Your PetMatch Account

⚠️ Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your PetMatch account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Your email address, password, and profile information
  • Pet Posts: All pet posts and photos you have uploaded
  • Messages: All chat messages and conversations
  • User Profile: Your display name and any profile information
  • App Activity: Your usage data and app interactions

What Happens to Your Data

Data Deletion Timeline:

  • Your account and data will be deleted immediately upon request
  • Some data may remain in our backup systems for up to 30 days for security and legal purposes
  • After 30 days, all data will be permanently removed from all systems

How to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps to delete your PetMatch account:

  1. Open the PetMatch app on your device
  2. Go to the Account/Profile tab (usually the bottom right icon)
  3. Scroll down and find the "Delete Account" or "Account Settings" option
  4. Tap "Delete Account" and confirm your decision
  5. Enter your password if prompted to verify your identity
  6. Confirm deletion - Your account will be permanently deleted
Note: If you cannot find the delete option in the app, or if you experience any issues, please contact us using the email below.

Alternative: Request Deletion via Email

If you prefer to request account deletion via email, or if you're unable to delete your account through the app:

Send an email to:

support@petmatch.com

Include the following information in your email:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your email address associated with the account
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

We will process your request within 7 business days.

Data Retention Policy

After you request account deletion:

  • Immediate deletion: Your account will be deactivated and most data removed immediately
  • Backup retention: Some data may remain in encrypted backups for up to 30 days for security and legal compliance purposes
  • Permanent removal: After 30 days, all data is permanently deleted from all systems, including backups
  • Legal requirements: We may retain certain information if required by law (e.g., transaction records for tax purposes)

What You Should Know

  • Once deleted, you cannot recover your account or any data
  • You will need to create a new account if you want to use PetMatch again
  • Your posts and messages will no longer be visible to other users
  • If you have any active conversations, they will be permanently deleted

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about account deletion or need assistance, please contact us:

Email: support@petmatch.com

We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
